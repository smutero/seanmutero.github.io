What are Ev's

What is a EV? This a question many people ask EV stands for electric vehicle in other terms it means a car that works runs on electricity basically An EV is defined as a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor that draws electricity from a battery and is capable of being charged from an external source and also An electric vehicle is a vehicle that uses one or more electric motors for propulsion. It can be powered by a collector system, with electricity from extravehicular sources, or it can be powered autonomously by a battery.
